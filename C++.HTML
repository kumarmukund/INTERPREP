WHAT IS C++ ?

C++ is a general purpose, case-sensitive, free-form programming language that supports object-oriented, procedural and generic programming.

HISTORY OF C++?

History of C++ language is interesting to know. Here we are going to discuss brief history of C++ language. C++ programming language was developed in 1980 by Bjarne Stroustrup at bell laboratories of AT&T (American Telephone & Telegraph), located in U.S.A. Bjarne Stroustrup is known as the founder of C++ language.
